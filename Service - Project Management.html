The Approach

A method,
in five movements.

We work the same way on every project — not because the work is repetitive, but because the discipline is. The room changes; the method is the constant.

01
One point of contact
From the first email to the final invoice, you speak to one senior. We do not pass projects between hands.
02
Weekly site walk
Every Wednesday. Photographs, snag log, programme update, and a single A4 page summarising progress, risk, and decisions needed.
03
The budget is a sketchbook
We hold the budget as a living document — open to you, reconciled monthly, and reported with the same care as the drawings.
04
Sample reviews, monthly
Physical reviews of every long-lead material, in our studio, with you, the contractor, and the joiner in the room. Decisions made out loud.
05
Handover, then aftercare
A dossier of every product, paint code, and maintenance schedule. Six months of aftercare — phone calls answered, snags resolved.
“A schedule is a promise made to a building. We do not break promises to buildings.”
